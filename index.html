FUTURES CONTRACT METHODOLOGY MASS-MARKET PREDICTIVE PRICING
Futures are priced contracts on mass-market American beers with sufficient historical data to model forward price.  ·  FORMULA: Weighted moving avg of all JWAL reviews + mean-reversion toward Untappd global avg (30%) + serving method adjustment  ·  CONTRACT EXECUTES automatically when a new review is entered for that beer — actual JWAL rating is compared to futures price  ·  BEAT: Actual ≥ Futures price  ·  MISS: Actual < Futures price
